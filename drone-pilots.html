Who should sign up?

If you own a drone and fly it for fun, it probably just sits there a lot of the time, and this is a way to do more with it in your spare time.

By signup up to be a Drone Pilot in Dronopy, you can do what you enjoy doing, and earn some money, which can be used to buy upgrades, or even a new drone.

You can post the types of work you can, and like to do, then people purchase that service from the site, and you arrange a time to perform the work. You can choose to charge for your services, or volunteer your time, and accept requests from worthy causes.

If you run a business that uses drones, you can also list the services you provide on dronopy.

Signing up and listing an unlimited amount of services is free.

How do i make a listing?

First, Sign Up! Then, you will need to enter your PayPal information (PayPal is the payment processor used, the payments will go into your PayPal account), and Post a Listing.

You can have as many as you’d like, one for each service. Think of a haircut price list… Haircut $20, Shave $10, Coloring $30, Haircut+Shave+Coloring $50. Each one would have it’s own listing and description.

Safety

Safety is the top priority, for you, and Drone Pilots.

If at any time you or the Pilot feels that something unsafe is happening, either party can put a stop to the activity, and Walk Away. This includes ‘stunt’ flying, flying too close to people, or violating the privacy of others.

Walk Away Guarantee

If you find your experience with the Drone Pilot to be unsatisfactory, for whatever reason, during the flight of the drone, you walk away at any time and receive a full refund. This is important to ensure that safety is followed at all times, if there is any concerns over the safety of the flight, or of the requests being unsafe, either side end the flight and walk away, without a problem.

Even with the great strides in technology and safety (some Drones come with backup flight systems for example), accidents are possible, and most are preventable, it is in everyone’s best interest to minimize the chance of it occurring.

Certification

Certification is an important legal requirement for piloting a drone for money in many areas. The regulations vary by country, and you are expected to abide by all local laws. Safety is always a top priority, and should you be asked to do something that seems dangerous, you are free to decline the request, and even Walk Away at any point in time.

Are there fees?

Signing up, and listing an unlimited amount of services is free. For each sale, there is a 4% transaction fee charged by the payment processor, and 15% commission for Dronopy.

The model we provide means that you can list as many services with us for free, and we only take a commission of the sales you get. This means you can set up your listings, and forget it, with no risk, or cost, while we do the work of finding the right customers for you. If no customers purchase your service, there is no cost to you, and if they do, we take a small fee for finding those customers.

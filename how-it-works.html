How it Works

Hiring a drone (pilot included) is like shopping for anything else online.

The drone marketplace lists the services available from local Drone Pilots, when you find what you’re looking for, checkout as you would at any other store. Once you checkout, a message will be sent to the Drone Pilot, who will confirm your order, and arrange a time with you to perform the service (think of it like arranging an appointment with a roofer, or plumber, or gardener).

Similar to other sites, Drone Pilots are rated based on the experiences of their buyers, so you can see the reputation of the Pilot, and how satisfied previous buyers were.

Dronopy is free to signup.

What Drones can do?

Drones are essentially flying robots, which have cameras or other sensors attached. The most common setup is for the drone to have a high-definition camera attached, in order to take high quality video, or pictures from the air.

Personal uses:

  • Video - High quality video of your home/property, aerial video from a distance of your special event, or video of activities with your sports activity (like skiing in the mountains)
  • Photo - Same as above, with HD or higher quality photos from the air. Selling your house? Some aerial photos can really make the listing stand out. Even 3D maps are possible.

Business uses:

  • Video - Create a series of videos showing a flyover of every hole of your golf course.
  • Photo - Create an aerial photo map of your property or farm, similar to google maps, and track changes over time.
  • Construction - Create photo maps and inspect sites from the air.
  • Volumetric Analysis, 3D Point Clouds, Multispectral and more...

Safety

Safety is the top priority, for you, and Drone Pilots.

If at any time you or the Pilot feels that something unsafe is happening, either party can put a stop to the activity, and Walk Away. This includes ‘stunt’ flying, flying too close to people, or violating the privacy of others.

Walk Away Guarantee

If you find your experience with the Drone Pilot to be unsatisfactory, for whatever reason, during the flight of the drone, you walk away at any time and receive a full refund. This is important to ensure that safety is followed at all times, if there is any concerns over the safety of the flight, or of the requests being unsafe, either side end the flight and walk away, without a problem.

Even with the great strides in technology and safety (some Drones come with backup flight systems for example), accidents are possible, and most are preventable, it is in everyone’s best interest to minimize the chance of it occurring.

All kinds of Drones

Personal

The Personal Drone Pilots are normal people, who enjoy piloting drones for personal enjoyment, have gained significant expertise by doing this, and are now offering that expertise to others. They do this in their spare time, and charge a fee.

Volunteer

The Volunteer Drone Pilots are offering their services for free to a good cause. They are normal people, who are donating their time, and the use of their drones, to help non-profits, church groups, parks services, and any other good cause.
